Experience Design

Experience design is the practice of designing products, services, media, information and interactions for humans. It is an extremely broad field that takes into account ideas, emotions, culture, language, aesthetics and anything else that influences human experience. Experiences can be designed to persuade, stimulate, entertain or inform.
